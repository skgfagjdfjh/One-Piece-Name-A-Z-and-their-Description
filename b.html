Bartolomeo

Bartolomeo the Cannibal is a Super Rookie, the captain of the Barto Club and the captain of the second ship of the Straw Hat Grand Fleet. He joined as a gladiator to compete for the Mera Mera no Mi at the Corrida Colosseum, where he met Monkey D. Luffy and pledged his loyalty towards him after winning the B Block.
